What happens when you initialize a repository? Why do you need to do it?

	Once the new repo is initialized and typed git status, it will show up the untracked files and also recommend you to do 'git add'.

How is the staging area different from the working directory and the repository? What value do you think it offers?

	Staging area is the stage where more than one untracked files from working directory can be added before making a single commit of them to repo
